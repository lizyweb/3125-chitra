About Us

Welcome to Chitraa Charitable Trust

Chitraa Charitable Trust is a non-profit organization registered under the Indian Trust Act, 1882, on 22nd April 2024. We are based in Tiruppur District, Tamil Nadu, India. Our trust is dedicated to empowering marginalized communities through sustainable development initiatives.

Our Core Values

Guiding Principles
Empowerment

Community Focus

Our Mission

To improve the lives of underprivileged individuals, especially women, children, and persons with disabilities, through comprehensive programs in healthcare, education, agriculture, and rehabilitation, ensuring affordable treatment and holistic development.

Our Vision

To create a society where everyone has access to quality healthcare, education, and economic opportunities, fostering a culture of inclusivity, equality, and social justice.

Our Objectives

What We Strive to Achieve

Healthcare Access

Provide affordable medical care, health education, and wellness programs, especially for marginalized communities.

Education for All

Support education initiatives, focusing on underprivileged children, and promoting literacy.

Empower Women

Enhance women's socio-economic status through skill development, livelihood training, and gender equality initiatives.

Disability Support

Provide rehabilitation services, accessibility initiatives, and inclusive opportunities for persons with disabilities.

Community Development

Foster community engagement, social cohesion, and collective progress.

Agricultural Development

Promote sustainable agricultural practices, improving farmers' livelihoods and food security.
